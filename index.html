Give a Day Global

We connect travelers with volunteer opportunities at reputable organizations around the world. Here's how it works:

  1. Sign up for a volunteer opportunity.
  2. Spend one day during your travels volunteering with one of our partner organizations.

We believe in human dignity

The best part of your trip can be a chance to make a difference.

Fiscal responsibility

Give a Day Global is a 510(c)(3) non-profit sponspored by The Silicon Valley Foundation. Every dollar you give goes directly to one of our partner organizations. We carefully vet our partner organizations to assess their effectiveness, integrity, and community impact.

Volunteer with our partners in South Africa

Baphumelele

A children's home located in a township 20 miles Southeast of Cape Town. Volunteer activies at Baphumelele in include:

  • Playing or reading books with the children in the kindergarten
  • Helping care for and comfort the sick in the medical programs
  • Cooking in their bakery and soup kitchen
  • Helping out in the woodshop
  • Working in the garden at their nearby farm

Masicorp

A community organization that operates out of Masiphumelele, Masicorp spearheads education initiatives and investment in enterprise so that the people there can improve their lives. Activies at this site include:

  • Volunteering at the community library
  • Working at the pre-school or primary school
  • Helping out with the Women's Life Skills program
