What volunteer activities do the partner organizations need help with?

Baphumelele

Baphumelele is a children's home in a township 20 miles Southeast of Cape Town. Volunteer activies at Baphumelele in include:

  • Playing or reading books with the children in the kindergarten
  • Helping care for and comfort the sick in the medical programs
  • Cooking in their bakery and soup kitchen
  • Helping out in the woodshop
  • Working in the garden at their nearby farm

Masicorp

Masicorp is a community organization that operates out of Masiphumelele. Masicorp spearheads education initiatives and investment in enterprise so that the people there can improve their lives. Activies at this site include helping in:

  • The community library
  • A pre-school or primary school
  • The Women's Life Skills program

Why Volunteer?

We believe in human dignity, that people should have access to safe water, healthy food, medical care, education, shelter, a healthy natural environment, and economic opportunities. By connecting travelers with nonprofit organizations who pursue these same goals, we aim to engage more people in making the world a better place, one day at a time.

Why choose us?

Give a day global is a Non Profit fiscally sponspored by the Silicon Valley Foundation. Every dollar you give goes directly to one of our partner organizations. We carefully vet our partner organizations to assess their effectiveness, integrity, and community impact.
